Get to know Gianna Pescada

How long have you been a performer?

2 Years

How would you describe your
on-stage persona and style?

Gianna Pescada is an eccentric, beautiful, acrobatic weirdo that most consider to be wanted in one county or another. She's a lovable mess that tucks and rolls into the spotlight and deathdrop into your heart/wallet. She is an extremely versatile bottom with the heart of a comedy queen, the face of a biological woman, and the moves of a Texas dancing diva who may or may not have had 3 and a half concussions. With her humble and "happy to be here" attitude, she's easily a fan favorite and a worthy contestant.

Why do you want to compete on The Queer Off?

Being selected to compete for the competition would give me the sense of validity and belonging within the drag community I've been seeking since first stepping onstage. Also, competing would no doubt challenge my drag and my ability to perform under pressure and stretch it to heights I've never imagined of reaching.

What is your Biggest pet peeve?

Using Teeth

Do you have a Favorite local performer?

Bleach

What is your favorite music to perform to?

Upbeat, dancey music with a message

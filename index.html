What is The Queer Off

The Queer Off is a new show, premiering August 1st 2018 at Sue Ellens in Dallas, Texas. It is a  12-week elimination style competition, showcasing the talents of the chosen contestants. The contest will have weekly scored judgings by guest judges. 14 contestants will chosen to battle week to week in runway and performance challenges while trying to avoid elimination. The last contestant standing will be awarded a prize package that includes, a crown, jewelry, and a cash prize!

How can I participate.

Participation in The Queer Off is in a casting basis. Prospective contestants can beging the audtion process by completing an application.

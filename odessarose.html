Get to know Odessa Rose

How long have you been a performer?

6

How would you describe your
on-stage persona and style?

Odessa Rose is a club kid drag queen who enjoys loud electronic music. However, my number one goal with every performance is make sure an emotion is conveyed to the audience. Whether that be done through dance or blood pouring out of my mouth, I am always thinking about how to create something provocative and exciting.

Why do you want to compete on The Queer Off?

Being in a competition like this is a great opportunity for me to get more exposure in Dallas, and hopefully introduce my drag to a lot of new people. As a newer drag queen I think it would also be a good way to be challenged and pushed out of my comfort zone.

What is your Biggest pet peeve?

Basic drag queens

Do you have a Favorite local performer?

May May Graves

What is your favorite music to perform to?

Grimes discography; anything electronic and sexual
